Things I Enjoy

Fencing

I'm an amateur fencer. More specifically, I practice HEMA, which stands for Historical European Martial Arts. Currently, I'm studying the German longsword tradition as laid down by historic masters, including Johannes Liechtenauer and Joachim Meyer. I'm also dabbling in Napoleonic saber based on texts by British duelist Charles Roworth.

I'm a proud member of my local club: San Diego Swordfighters. If it interests you, consider checking it out.
